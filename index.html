International Virtual Conference on Interdisciplinary Development in Science, Technology, and Humanities (IVCIDSTH-2020, 05-06, August, 2020) an online conference is a specialized conference on interdisciplinary development in Science, Technology, and Humanities. The scope of IVCIDSTH-2020 conference covers theoretic and applied interdisciplinary development in Science, Technology, and Humanities. It aims, in particular, at fostering cooperation among practitioners and theoreticians in this fields.

About Jaywanti Haksar Govt. P. G. College ðŸ”—

Jaywanti Haksar Govt. Post-Graduate College, Betul, MP, India is accredited with B+ level by national assessment and accreditation council (NAAC) Bangalore. College has been imparting education to graduate, post graduate and Ph. D. research scholar in Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry, Biotechnology, Microbiology, Botany, Zoology, Computer, Arts and Commerce. It was established in the year 1957. College has ensured overall socio-economic growth of all the sections of society by encouraging greater access and inclusive approach making it most preferred institution for higher education, learning and research. The college has always felt concerned about increasing access of students from various sections of the society to higher education. By providing reservations, financial aids, scholarships and relaxation in qualifications for socially backward classes. The college aims to achieve its goal of providing higher education to create just, plural and equitable society in consonance with constitutional values.

About Chhindwara University ðŸ”—

Chhindwara University is newly established university in Madhya Pradesh. It has been established in the year of 2019 and contains 132 colleges (government and non-government). University covers almost full spectrum of higher education, offering courses in its affiliated Colleges, Teaching Departments in the faculties of Arts, Social Science, Science, Life Science, Law, Commerce, Education, Physical Education, Technology, Home Science and Management. Territorial jurisdiction of the university extends to four districts namely Chhindwara, Seoni, Balaghat and Betul. Ever since its inception university has been striving to maintain excellence in teaching, research and community service. Great emphasis has laid in creating scientific temper, maintaining high ethical values and in keeping pace with emerging areas of higher learning.
